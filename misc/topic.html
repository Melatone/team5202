Computer Vision

Computer Vision in short is the process of using AI or Deep-Learning to provide a computerized program "sight" in a more human sense of the word. This sense of sight allows a computer to discern whats physcially in a photo or video and/or any other pertinent information about what the image is rather than being able to return irrelevant information such as pixel-by-pixel information or anything else contained in a photo or videos metadata. It's application holds its ties into the computerization and optimization of many pre-existing processes relating to security and ease-of-life.
